# Product Guide: Course Transcript Downloader & Ebook Generator

## Overview
A specialized tool designed to assist students in downloading lecture transcripts from the Campus IL course website and transforming them into a structured, reading-first study ebook.

## Target Audience
- **Students:** Individuals enrolled in the "Viruses" course on Campus IL who prefer studying from text-based materials and desire a cohesive textbook for exam preparation.

## Core Features
- **Bulk Download:** Capability to fetch all available course transcripts in a single execution.
- **Metadata Retention:** Saved files include lecture titles and chapter names.
- **Ebook Transformation:** Synthesize raw transcripts into a structured, textbook-style Hebrew ebook with a logical chapter flow.
- **Study Supports:** Each chapter includes learning objectives, high-yield summaries, key terms, and practice questions (MCQs and short answer).
- **Consolidated Materials:** Integrated glossary, question bank, and exam review sections.
- **Plain Text & Markdown Output:** Transcripts are saved as `.txt` files; the ebook is generated in Markdown format, ready for conversion to EPUB or PDF.

## Technical Constraints & Requirements
- **Authentication:** The tool must handle user authentication to access restricted course content.
- **Campus IL Integration:** Specifically tailored to navigate and extract data from the Campus IL platform.
